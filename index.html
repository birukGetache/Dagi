Our Ekub Features

  • Community Savings

    Ekub unites individuals in a community-driven savings plan, where members contribute and receive funds on a rotating basis.

  • Lending & Borrowing

    Members can borrow from the pooled savings when needed, ensuring access to funds while promoting equal participation.

  • Trust & Security

    Trust is at the core of Ekub. Members are accountable for their contributions, ensuring a secure and reliable system.

  • Rotating Funds

    Ekub operates on a rotating fund model, where each member receives a lump sum at different intervals, fostering fairness and equality.
